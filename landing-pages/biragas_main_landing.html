The Epistemological Crisis in Drug Discovery

Why do drug candidates keep failing? The answer lies in how we evaluate evidence.

$2.6B

Average cost to bring one drug to market

90%

Of drug candidates fail in clinical trials

70%

The root cause: the industry relies on correlational evidence that doesn't distinguish causes from consequences. Correlational biomarkers appear significant in discovery but fail in the clinic because association is not causation.
