# 1. CAMPAIGN STRATEGY OVERVIEW

## Objective
Position BiRAGAS as the first AI platform that delivers **causal** (not correlational) intelligence for drug discovery, biomarker validation, and therapeutic target prioritization — driving qualified leads from pharma, biotech, and academic institutions.

## Core Messaging Pillars

| Pillar | Message | Supporting Evidence |
|--------|---------|-------------------|
| **Causality Over Correlation** | "Stop chasing correlations. Start proving causation." | 7-phase pipeline, Mendelian Randomization, Pearl's do-calculus |
| **Hallucination-Free AI** | "AI you can trust — 5-layer hallucination detection." | Phase 3 dual-gate system, 5 hallucination categories, LLM arbitration |
| **Patient-Level Precision** | "Every patient gets their own causal graph." | Patient-level DAG instantiation, PSI framework, cohort stratification |
| **End-to-End Pipeline** | "From raw omics to drug targets in one platform." | 23 modules, 7 phases, automated DAG construction to clinical reports |
| **Validated & Transparent** | "Every edge, every claim — fully auditable." | Evidence inspection, provenance tracking, FDA-aligned reporting |

## Brand Voice
- **Authoritative** — grounded in peer-reviewed science (18 publications)
- **Disruptive** — challenging the status quo of correlational bioinformatics
- **Precise** — specific numbers, specific methods, no vague AI hype
- **Accessible** — complex science communicated clearly for decision-makers
